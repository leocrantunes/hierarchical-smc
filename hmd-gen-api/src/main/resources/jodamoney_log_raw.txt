    Change BigMoney to normalize negative scales
    
    Ensure that a negative scale is not visible in BigMoney
    This should have no impact on most users, but does change semantics
    The rationale is to ensure that the toString() matches equals()
    and the internal state as per VALJO rules
    
    Previously,
     BigMoney.ofScale(GBP, 100, 0)
     BigMoney.ofScale(GBP, 1, -2)
    were equal but had the same toString()
    Now, they are both normalized to the first form - no negative scale
    Fixes #52
